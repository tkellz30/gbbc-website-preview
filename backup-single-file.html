Welcome

You Belong Here

We're so glad you're here — whether you're exploring faith or growing deeper in your walk with God. Grace is a Bible-driven, Christ-centered church where lives are being changed by God's Word. We're excited about our new church building and the fresh season of growth and fellowship God has prepared. You'll find a welcoming, joyful community of real people who love God, love others, and want to grow together. Wherever you are on your spiritual journey — you belong here.

Grace Bible Baptist Church is located at 7201 Klondike Rd, Pensacola, FL 32526 — the white building in the back. We meet every Sunday and Wednesday and would love to have you join us.

What We Believe

Mission & Beliefs

Grace Bible Baptist Church is committed to the authority of Scripture and the centrality of Jesus Christ in all things.

We believe the Old and New Testaments to be verbally inspired by God, inerrant in the original writings, and that they are of supreme and final authority in faith and life. II Timothy 3:16–17; II Peter 1:19–21
We believe the Godhead eternally exists in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. These three are one God, having precisely the same nature, attributes, and perfections. Genesis 1:1, 1:26; Matthew 13:14; Romans 8:9

What to Expect

You're Going to Love It Here

No dress code, no performance, no pressure. Just come.

  1. 1
    Park & Be Greeted

    Our greeters will meet you from the moment you pull in. Plenty of parking available.

  2. 2
    Children's Sunday School at 9:45 AM

    Drop the kids off in our safe, caring children's class before the worship service.

  3. 3
    Worship Service at 10:45 AM

    Expect praise, prayer, and expository preaching rooted in Scripture. Service runs about 60–75 minutes.

  4. 4
    Stop by the Welcome Center

    After service, meet our staff and members. Grab a visitor gift. No pressure — just love.

  5. 5
    Wednesday Nights at 6:45 PM

    Our mid-week Prayer Service is a quieter, intimate gathering for prayer and Bible study. All are welcome.

Building Project Update

Our New Home on
Fairfield Drive

God is doing something wonderful at Grace — and you're a part of it.

The Story

A Season of Growth and Faith

For years, Grace Bible Baptist Church has called 7201 Klondike Rd home — meeting faithfully in the white building in the back, trusting God for the future. That trust is now bearing fruit.

We are in the middle of an exciting building project at 4805 W Fairfield Drive, Pensacola — a new permanent facility that will allow us to serve our congregation and community more effectively for generations to come.

This project is possible only because of the faithfulness of God and the generosity of this church family. Every prayer, every gift, every hour volunteered is a brick in this building — and a testimony to what God can do through willing hearts.

"For my thoughts are not your thoughts, neither are your ways my ways, saith the Lord."

— Isaiah 55:8–9 (KJV)

We covet your prayers as we move forward. If you'd like to give specifically to the Building Fund, you can do so online through ChurchTrac. And if you'd like to see the site or learn more, please reach out to us — we'd love to share the vision with you in person.
